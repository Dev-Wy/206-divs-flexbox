When you are initially trying to calm your child down from a spiral, get their attention.

Whisper, rather than yell so they are seeking your sound.
Once they are making eye contact, let them know what you two are about to do together.

Ask them to sense where they feel different in their body when they are having their big emotion.
Guide them through letting go of that feeling with a 4 seconds inhale, 3 secondsS to hold, and 5 second exhale.

Repeat this until the pressure goes away.
